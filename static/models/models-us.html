The first three digits of the chassis number specify the general body style (i.e.123). The next digit generally indicates gas (0) or Diesel (1) - this rule has certainly been violated. The next two digits indicate the specific version of the body style. In the case of 123s, if the last two digits are 9X a wagon is indicated.
